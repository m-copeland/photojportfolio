About Me

I'm an award-winning journalist and writer with a passion for storytelling. I have a strong background in writing and am driven by my desire to embrace creativity and connection through writing.

My passion lies with magazine and long-form writing; I am also accomplished in everything from columns to news to creative writing. I have experience with social media and digital mediums from both writing and marketing perspectives. I'm passionate about exploring the potential for written journalism to expand into different frameworks and multimedia methods.
